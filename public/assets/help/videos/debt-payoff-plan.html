Debt Avalanche

Best for: Maximum interest savings

Method: Pay minimum on all debts, then extra payments to highest interest rate first

Advantage: Mathematically optimal approach that minimizes total interest paid

Debt Snowball

Best for: Psychological wins and motivation

Method: Pay minimum on all debts, then extra payments to smallest balance first

Advantage: Quick wins build momentum and motivation throughout the process
